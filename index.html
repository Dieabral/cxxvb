Our Mission

Supporting Business Efficiency

Located in Itabirito, Minas Gerais, FINANCE PROMOTORA DE VENDAS LTDA is a dedicated provider of business support services. Under the leadership of Diego Alexandre Rodrigues Cabral, we specialize in streamlining administrative functions for companies of all sizes.

We operate within the "Administrative and Support Services" sector, offering a range of solutions from office administration to adjustment and collection services. Our goal is to reduce the operational burden on our clients, allowing them to allocate resources more effectively.

Whether you need assistance with back-office management or specialized support in debt collection, our team delivers professional, compliant, and results-driven service.
